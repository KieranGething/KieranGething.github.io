Kieran J. Gething

Post-doctoral Research Fellow
Loughborough University

Kieran Gething is an ecologist who studies drying streams and ditches. His research focuses on the aquatic and terrestrial invertebrates that use drying streams and ditches at different stages of their wet–dry cycle. The aim of his work is to develop tools to monitor and assess ecosystem health.

Since 2021, Kieran has also been a part-time lecturer at NTU, contributing to teaching on Environmental Science and GIS modules.
